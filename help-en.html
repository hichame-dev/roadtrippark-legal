How do I follow a road trip?
RoadTripPark offers turnkey road trips, already designed and tested. Here's how to launch one:

1. Choose your road trip. From the Explore tab, browse available itineraries. The Costa Dorada road trip is free; the others require the Roadtrip Pass or the annual subscription.

2. Open the detailed page. You'll find the stages, distances, total duration, parkings, campsites and points of interest.

3. Launch the road trip. Tap Start this road trip. A confirmation window appears. Once launched, the road trip becomes active in the Roadtrip tab.

4. Follow the route in real time. Your GPS position is displayed on the map. When you arrive within 1 km of a stage, the app sends you a notification and automatically switches the display to that stage.

5. Validate the stage on site. Once on site, tap Complete this stage and confirm. The app checks your GPS position to validate proximity. If you are too far away, validation is refused.

6. Capture your trip. Take photos from the Gallery tab by creating a dedicated album for your trip. You will then find them in the road trip's travel journal.

7. Finish the road trip. Once the last stage is validated, you can mark the road trip as completed and review your entire journey in the travel journal.
How do I add photos to my trip?
Open the Gallery tab, create an album (or open an existing one), then tap the add button to import a photo from your library or take one with the camera.

Photos are organised by album, not directly linked to a specific stage. You can then find them in the road trip's travel journal.
How does offline mode work?
When you lose connection, a red banner appears at the top of the screen to warn you. Images you have already viewed remain visible thanks to the automatic cache.

To have a road trip fully usable offline, open its page from the Explore tab and launch the manual download via the caching modal. This feature requires the Roadtrip Pass or the annual subscription.
How do I edit my profile?
Profile elements are edited in two places:

Profile picture and bio: in the Profile tab. Tap your photo to change it, and tap the bio area to edit it directly.

Username (display name): in Settings > Profile > Username. A window lets you edit it.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete my account. Confirm by entering your password.

Deletion is immediate and irreversible: your account, photos, road trips, friends and all associated data are erased without delay.
Is the app free?
RoadTripPark offers three plans:

Free: access to the Costa Dorada road trip, live map and stage validation enabled.
Roadtrip Pass (€7.90): 1-year access to ONE road trip of your choice. One-off purchase via Google Play, no auto-renewal.
Annual Subscription (€39.90/year): 1-year access to the 27 road trips in the catalogue. Tacit annual renewal, cancellable at any time.

When the plan expires, the road trip and its live map are locked again. Your profile, gallery and replay map remain accessible.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Annual Subscription (€39.90/year): tacit renewal. To cancel, go to Google Play Store > Subscriptions > RoadTripPark > Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current period: you keep access until the expiration date.

Roadtrip Pass (€7.90): one-off purchase with no renewal (Non-Renewing Subscription). No cancellation is needed: access ends automatically at the end of the year, with no further charge.
How do I share my road trip with friends?
You can make your road trip public or private from the page of the trip in progress. When public, your friends can view your travel journal and photos.

To add and manage your friends, open the Friends tab from your profile. This feature requires the Roadtrip Pass or the annual subscription.

You can also share the video montage of your trip from the Video montage screen, via your phone's native sharing options.

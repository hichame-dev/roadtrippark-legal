Find answers to your questions

How do I create a road trip?
From the Roadtrip tab, tap the "+" button to create a new trip. Add a title, a description and your stages. You can also start from a community road trip in the Explore tab.
How do I add photos to my trip?
Open your road trip, then go to the gallery. You can take a photo directly or import one from your phone gallery. Photos are linked to the current stage.
How does offline mode work?
Images you have already viewed are automatically cached on your device. Mapbox map tiles are also cached. A banner lets you know when you have no internet connection. Full offline mode is available with the Roadtrip Pass or Premium subscription.
How do I edit my profile?
Go to the Profile tab and tap the edit button. You can change your photo, display name and bio.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Profile > Delete my account. This action is irreversible and will delete all your data, photos and road trips within 30 days.
Is the app free?
RoadTripPark offers three plans:

Free: 1 free road trip (Costa Dorada), gallery limited to 30 photos, GPS enabled.
Roadtrip Pass (€9.90): permanent purchase of a road trip + 3-month Premium access (100 photos, friends, offline mode).
Premium (€29.90/year): all road trips, unlimited gallery, video montage, priority support.
How do I cancel my subscription?
The Premium subscription renews automatically each year. To cancel:

Android: Google Play Store > Subscriptions > RoadTripPark > Cancel.
iOS: Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > RoadTripPark > Cancel.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current period. You keep Premium access until the expiration date.
How do I share my road trip with friends?
From your road trip page, tap the share button. You can invite friends to join your trip or share a public link to your travel journal. The friends feature is available with the Roadtrip Pass or Premium subscription.

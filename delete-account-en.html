Delete my account

Last updated: April 2026

Version francaise

In accordance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), you can delete your RoadTripPark account and all your personal data at any time.

Data deleted

Deleting your account permanently erases:

  • User profile: name, email, profile picture, cover photo, preferences
  • Photos and albums: all gallery photos and their files (Amazon S3)
  • Trips: progress, completed stages, distance traveled, statistics
  • Comments and likes
  • Friends: friends list, sent and received friend requests
  • Notifications
  • Badges and trophies
  • Subscription: Premium or Roadtrip Pass (refunds must be requested through Google Play or the App Store)
  • Device tokens (push notifications)
  • Blocked users
  • Authentication account (Firebase)

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to delete my account

  1. Open the RoadTripPark app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom right tab)
  3. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  5. Tap Delete account (red button)
  6. Confirm the deletion
  7. If your account is linked to an email: enter your password
  8. If your account is linked to Google: confirm via the Google popup

Deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email. Send your request to contact@roadtrippark.com with the email address associated with your account.

Processing time: 72 hours maximum.

Active subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your store subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription in the Google Play or App Store settings before deleting your account.

Delete my account

Last updated: April 2026

Version francaise

In accordance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), you can delete your RoadTripPark account and all your personal data at any time.

Data deleted

Deleting your account permanently erases:

  • User profile: name, email, profile picture, cover photo, preferences
  • Photos and albums: all gallery photos and their files (Amazon S3)
  • Trips: progress, completed stages, distance traveled, statistics
  • GPS traces (breadcrumbs): all geolocation points recorded during your road trips
  • Comments and likes
  • Friends: friends list, sent and received friend requests
  • Notifications
  • Badges and trophies
  • Paid access: Roadtrip Pass or Annual Subscription (refunds must be requested through Google Play)
  • Device tokens (push notifications)
  • Blocked users
  • Authentication account (Firebase)

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to delete my account

  1. Open the RoadTripPark app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom right tab)
  3. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  5. Tap Delete account (red button)
  6. Confirm the deletion
  7. If your account is linked to an email: enter your password
  8. If your account is linked to Google: confirm via the Google popup

Deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email. Send your request to contact@roadtrippark.com with the email address associated with your account.

Processing time: 72 hours maximum.

Active Annual Subscription and Roadtrip Pass

Annual Subscription (€39.90/year): deleting your account does not automatically cancel the renewal on the store. To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription in Google Play settings before deleting your account.

Roadtrip Pass (€7.90): one-off purchase with no renewal (Non-Renewing Subscription). No store action is required: no further charge can occur.

Delete only part of your data

You can individually delete certain data from the app, without deleting your account:

  • Photos: open the photo → Delete button
  • Albums: gallery → album options → Delete
  • Comments: long press on the comment → Delete
  • Friends: friend's profile → Remove from friends

Retention period after deletion

Your data is deleted immediately from our active servers. Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days maximum. Data related to transactions (Roadtrip Pass and annual subscription purchases) is retained for 10 years in accordance with legal accounting obligations.
